Here is a description of the data:

T (60001 x 1): time
Y (60001 x 1): head position
U (60001 x 1): voltage

The sampling frequency is 4000 Hz, but different authors resampled at lower frequencies. For instance, Juloski resampled at 50 Hz, while Paoletti resampled at 150 Hz.
